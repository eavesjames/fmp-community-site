## What it is

Cisco announces the Silicon One G300 switching silicon and new N9000/8000 systems designed for AI data center buildouts. The announcement highlights 102.4 Tbps switching speeds, 100% liquid cooling options that enable nearly 70% energy efficiency improvements, and enhanced Nexus One management for simplified operations across hyperscalers to enterprises.
