## What it is

ECA Ontario is hosting a free hybrid training event focused on Fault Managed Power Systems and Digital Electricity technology. This educational session aims to introduce electrical professionals to FMPS as an emerging technology, helping build awareness and knowledge within the Ontario electrical contractor community.

## Why it matters

Industry events provide networking opportunities and access to latest technical updates. Conference presentations often preview upcoming developments before formal announcements.
