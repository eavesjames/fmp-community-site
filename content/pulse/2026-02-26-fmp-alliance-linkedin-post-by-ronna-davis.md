## What it is

Ronna Davis, Secretary of FMP Alliance, announces a free online training clinic in March 2025 for professionals interested in learning about fault managed power systems. The clinic will cover codification, safety standards, and design architectures, responding to strong interest from the BICSI Winter event in Orlando about FMP training opportunities.
